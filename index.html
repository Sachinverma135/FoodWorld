Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish made of spicy chickpea curry (chole) served with deep-fried, fluffy bread (bhature). Known for its rich flavors and hearty appeal, it’s a favorite street food and comfort meal. Often paired with pickles, onions, and a glass of lassi, it’s a treat for food lovers.

Vada Pav

Vada Pav is a popular Mumbai street food featuring a spicy potato fritter in a bun. It's served with chutneys and fried chilies, offering a quick, flavorful snack.

Paneer Biryani

Paneer Biryani is a flavorful Indian rice dish cooked with marinated paneer, aromatic spices, and herbs. It's a vegetarian favorite, often enjoyed with raita.
